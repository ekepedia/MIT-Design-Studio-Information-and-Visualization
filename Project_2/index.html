Weather App

Mocks

I wanted my weather app to highlight what I thought was the most important information a user would need to know when checking the weather: the temperature at different moments of the day, and the overall direction of the weather throughout the week.

My first app reduces 24 hours into 4 discreet time periods that I thought were the most useful. The bar goes does the screen revealing the weather as it goes on—though, the other times are still viewable.

My second app is a variation of a bar graph turned sideway that shows the highs and lows as well as the variance in the weather during the week.
